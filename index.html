soccer-on-a-globe

Ever been interested in a soccer team, but not been sure where they are located or play?

Now, with the power of CesiumJS, you can see where all of your favorite clubs play, with Soccer-on-a-globe!

Select a team from the league-based dropdown in the upper-left, and the camera will zoom to the stadium where they play. Want to know more about the team? A box will appear to give you that information when the team is selected.

The page might be limited in scope now, but if you want to contribute more information about a team, suggest a league to add, or add your expertise to the project, clone the repository or submit an issue and I will do what I can to make this a better tool for soccer fans worldwide!
